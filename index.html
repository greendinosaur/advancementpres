Research Questions

  1. What tools do remote software teams use to collaborate and how do they choose which to use?
  2. How do remote software teams use tools to collaborate?
  3. What challenges do they face with their tooling?

Research Method

Semi-structured interviews by a pair of researchers with 8 participants

Recruited from researchers' personal networks

Transcripts reviewed and memo per interview. Collated to identify common themes. Reviewed with a second researcher

RQ3: What challenges do they face with their tooling?

Information silos

I’ve been working with Google Drive for quite some time now and I still can’t figure out sometimes where documents are, where they go and how to find them. So, typically, the best way that I find documents that are shared through Google Docs is through my Slack notification saying someone shared a document with you and I can just go and look exactly for that document, click on the link and go, rather than searching Google Drive for it.

RQ3: What challenges do they face with their tooling?

Information overload

I mean that's kind of my biggest gripe about communicating that way is always just having to have Slack open and getting notifications for all the messages that I may not necessarily care about at the moment when I try to focus on something else.
